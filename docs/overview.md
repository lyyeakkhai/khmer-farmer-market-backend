# Khmer Farmer Market — Project Overview

## What We're Building

Khmer Farmer Market is a digital platform that empowers Cambodian farmers to sell their agricultural products directly to local and international customers — no middlemen, no unfair pricing, no barriers.

We're transforming Cambodia's traditional farming and trading practices into a modern, technology-driven marketplace. The result: farmers earn more, buyers get fresher goods, and Cambodian agriculture gets the global recognition it deserves.

---

## The Problem

Cambodia's agricultural supply chain is dominated by intermediaries who capture most of the value. Farmers work hard but earn little. Their products rarely reach international markets, and when they do, the margins are thin. Traditional selling methods limit scale, transparency, and income potential.

---

## Our Solution

A direct-to-consumer digital marketplace where:

- Farmers list, manage, and price their own products
- Buyers — local or international — purchase directly from the source
- The supply chain becomes transparent, efficient, and fair
- Cambodian agricultural goods gain higher value and global visibility

---

## Core Purpose

| Goal | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Remove middlemen | Farmers keep more of what they earn |
| Direct-to-consumer selling | Farmers control their products and pricing |
| Global market access | Reach international buyers beyond Cambodia |
| Digitize agriculture | Scale traditional commerce into a sustainable system |

---

## Vision

> To empower Cambodian farmers by transforming traditional agriculture into a modern digital marketplace — enabling direct sales, fair pricing, and global reach.

---

## Long-Term Impact

This isn't just an e-commerce platform. It's a movement to:

- Strengthen Cambodia's agricultural economy
- Promote "Made in Cambodia" products worldwide
- Build a sustainable, inclusive digital ecosystem for farmers
- Inspire the next generation to adopt technology in agriculture

---

## Who We Serve

- **Farmers** — smallholder and commercial farmers across Cambodia looking for better market access and fairer income
- **Local buyers** — consumers and businesses who want fresh, traceable Cambodian produce
- **International buyers** — importers and distributors seeking authentic Cambodian agricultural goods
